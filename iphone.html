About iPhone

Launched in 2007, the iPhone revolutionized the smartphone industry and set a new standard for mobile devices. It combined a phone, an iPod, and an internet communicator into a single device, introducing the concept of a touchscreen interface that would become the hallmark of smartphones. The original iPhone featured a 3.5-inch screen, a 2-megapixel camera, and 4 or 8 GB of storage, which was groundbreaking at the time.
