Apple's USB-C Magic Accessories: A Step Toward Universal Compatibility

Apple's latest Magic Keyboard, Trackpad, and Mouse now feature USB-C, allowing for universal compatibility across Apple's product lineup. With fast charging and reliable connectivity, these new peripherals complement the recently updated iMac series.

Apple's shift toward USB-C standardization is a move to simplify user experiences and ensure cross-device functionality. These accessories are available in multiple colors to match the iMac's vibrant design.
