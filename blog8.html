Apple Music and AirPods Pro: Personalized Sound, Adaptive Audio

Apple Music now offers personalized playlists, tailored recommendations, and live station curation. Meanwhile, AirPods Pro introduce Adaptive Audio, adjusting volume levels automatically to surrounding environments.

This combination enhances listening experiences by adapting to user preferences and conditions, making music and audio immersive and intuitive.
