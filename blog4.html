Apple's New 24-Inch iMac: Power, Color, and M4 Performance

The updated 24-inch iMac combines Apple's M4 chip with vibrant color options, bringing high performance to an elegant desktop design. This new iMac is 1.7x faster than its predecessor and offers improved graphics for video and photo editing.

New features include a 12MP camera with Center Stage for video calls, color-matched accessories, and advanced audio capabilities. Priced from $1,299, the iMac delivers top-notch performance in an aesthetic package, ideal for both home offices and creative workspaces.
